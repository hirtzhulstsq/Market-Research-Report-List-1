<p><ul><li>Real-wood Floor</li><li>Engineered Floor</li><li>Laminated Floor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hardwood flooring market comprises three main types: real-wood floors, engineered floors, and laminated floors. Real-wood floors are made from solid timber, offering longevity and natural beauty. Engineered floors consist of multiple layers, combining real wood veneer with high-quality plywood, providing enhanced stability and moisture resistance. Laminated floors, made from composite materials, mimic the appearance of wood while being more affordable and easier to maintain. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in style, durability, and budget.</p></p>
