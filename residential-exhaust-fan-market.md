<p><p>The residential exhaust fan market includes three primary types: ceiling fans, wall-mounted fans, and window-mounted fans. Ceiling fans are designed to circulate air through the entire room, providing a comfortable airflow and reducing temperature. Wall-mounted fans are ideal for smaller spaces, delivering targeted ventilation while saving floor space. Window-mounted fans are installed in open windows, facilitating cross-ventilation and enhancing air circulation throughout the home. Each type offers unique benefits suited to different residential needs and preferences.</p></p>
