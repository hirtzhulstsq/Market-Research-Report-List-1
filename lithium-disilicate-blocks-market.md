<p><p>Lithium disilicate blocks are categorized into three translucency types based on their optical properties and intended dental applications. High translucency blocks offer superior aesthetic qualities, making them ideal for anterior restorations where natural appearance is paramount. Medium translucency blocks strike a balance between aesthetics and strength, suitable for both anterior and posterior applications. Low translucency blocks provide increased strength and are often used in posterior restorations where aesthetics are less critical, emphasizing durability over appearance.</p></p>
