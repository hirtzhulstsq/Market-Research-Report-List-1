<p><p>The Wedge Wire Filter market is categorized into two main types: Tube Type Wedge Wire Filters and Screen Type Wedge Wire Filters. Tube Type filters are cylindrical, designed for applications requiring high flow rates and minimal pressure drop, making them ideal for liquid filtration. In contrast, Screen Type Wedge Wire Filters feature flat panels, providing a larger surface area for effective solid separation and are commonly used in wastewater treatment and mining industries. Both types enhance efficiency and durability in filtration processes.</p></p>
