<p><p>The Celiac Test market comprises three main types: serologic tests, genetic testing, and small-bowel biopsy. Serologic tests detect specific antibodies in the blood, indicating an immune response to gluten. Genetic testing identifies the presence of specific genes associated with celiac disease, providing insights into predisposition. Small-bowel biopsy, considered the gold standard, involves taking a tissue sample from the intestine to identify damage caused by gluten consumption. Together, these methods help diagnose celiac disease and guide treatment strategies.</p></p>
