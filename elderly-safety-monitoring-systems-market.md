<p><p>Elderly Safety Monitoring Systems can be categorized into two main types: automatic alarms and active alarms. Automatic alarms are designed to trigger alerts without any manual intervention, typically using sensors that detect falls or emergencies. These systems provide peace of mind for caregivers and family members by ensuring rapid response in critical situations. On the other hand, active alarms require user initiation, allowing elderly individuals to manually alert caregivers in case of distress. Both types enhance safety and independence for seniors.</p></p>
