<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Base Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Group I</li><li>Group II</li><li>Group III</li><li>Group IV</li><li>Group V</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Base oils are categorized into five groups based on their production processes and properties. Group I consists of solvent-refined oils with higher sulfur and viscosity. Group II oils are hydrotreated, offering better performance and lower sulfur content. Group III oils are further refined, providing superior stability and lower volatility. Group IV includes synthetic oils, typically polyalphaolefins, known for their exceptional performance. Group V encompasses all other base oils, including esters and biologically derived products, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
