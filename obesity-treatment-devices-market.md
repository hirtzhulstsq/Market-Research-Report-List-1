<p><p>The obesity treatment devices market comprises various types aimed at promoting weight loss. Gastric banding systems involve placing a band around the stomach to restrict food intake. Gastric balloon systems utilize an inflatable balloon in the stomach to induce a feeling of fullness. Gastric electrical stimulation systems deliver electrical pulses to inhibit hunger signals. Gastric emptying systems accelerate food transit from the stomach, while gastric aspiration systems remove food directly from the stomach post-consumption. Other devices may include newer or less common treatments.</p></p>
