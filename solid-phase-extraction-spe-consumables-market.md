<p><p>The Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) consumables market is categorized into three main types: SPE cartridges, SPE disks, and other specialized products. SPE cartridges are cylindrical tubes filled with sorbent material, designed for efficient liquid sample processing. SPE disks are flat, disk-shaped devices that provide a larger surface area for extraction, enhancing sample throughput. Other types may include mini-columns or pipette tips with integrated sorbents, catering to specific applications in analytical chemistry and environmental testing.</p></p>
