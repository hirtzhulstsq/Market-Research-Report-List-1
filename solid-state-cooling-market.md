<p><p>The solid-state cooling market encompasses three main types: single-stage, multi-stage, and thermocycler systems. Single-stage cooling units are compact and efficient for moderate temperature control applications. Multi-stage systems offer enhanced cooling capabilities by using multiple thermoelectric modules, ideal for more demanding tasks requiring significant temperature differentials. Thermocyclers, commonly used in laboratories, provide precise temperature cycling for biochemical processes, making them essential in research and clinical labs for applications like PCR. Each type serves distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
