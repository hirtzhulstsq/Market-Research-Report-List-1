<p><p>The sustainable plastic packaging market is categorized into two main types: rigid and flexible. Rigid packaging includes containers like bottles and boxes that maintain shape and structure, often made from biodegradable materials. It is ideal for products requiring durability and protection. Flexible packaging, on the other hand, encompasses items such as pouches and wraps that can easily adapt to the product's shape, promoting material efficiency. Both types aim to reduce environmental impact while offering functionality and convenience in product packaging.</p></p>
