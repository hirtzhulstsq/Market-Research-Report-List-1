<p><p>The electrical resistance wire market is primarily categorized into two types: round type and flat type. Round type wires are cylindrical and commonly used for applications requiring flexibility and uniform heating, making them ideal for high-temperature resistance and wire-wound applications. In contrast, flat type wires, which are ribbon-like, offer increased surface area for better heat dissipation and are often utilized in applications requiring tight spaces or specific heating profiles, such as heating elements in appliances and industrial equipment.</p></p>
