<p><p>The Rapid Diagnostic Kits market includes various types of testing methodologies. Lateral flow tests are popular for their ease of use and quick results, typically employing a strip format. Agglutination assays utilize particle aggregation for detecting specific antigens or antibodies, allowing for visual interpretation of results. Flow-through tests involve the movement of liquid through a solid substrate, enhancing sensitivity. Solid phase methods involve immobilizing antigens or antibodies on a solid surface, enabling detailed analysis and quantification of samples.</p></p>
