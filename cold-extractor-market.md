<p><p>The Cold Extraction market encompasses two primary types: Physical Extraction and Chemical Extraction. Physical Extraction relies on methods like cold pressing or mechanical extraction, preserving the integrity of the extracted materials by utilizing low temperatures. Conversely, Chemical Extraction involves the use of solvents and chemicals to separate desired compounds from raw materials, often enhancing yield but potentially altering the final product's purity and properties. Both methods serve distinct applications across industries such as food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
