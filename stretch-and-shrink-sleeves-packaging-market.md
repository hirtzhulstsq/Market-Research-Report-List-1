<p><p>Stretch and shrink sleeve packaging markets encompass three primary printing types: flexography, digital printing, and rotogravure. Flexography utilizes flexible relief plates for high-speed production, ideal for large runs with vibrant colors. Digital printing offers customization and quicker turnaround for smaller batches, leveraging digital technology for precise designs. Rotogravure, known for its high-quality output, involves engraved cylinders, making it suitable for long runs with intricate details. Each method caters to specific packaging needs, influencing branding and product visibility.</p></p>
