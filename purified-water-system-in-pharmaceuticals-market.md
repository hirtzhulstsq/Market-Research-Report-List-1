<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange System</li><li>RO System</li><li>Distillation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The purified water system in the pharmaceuticals market comprises various technologies, including Ion Exchange Systems, Reverse Osmosis (RO) Systems, and Distillation Systems. Ion Exchange Systems remove impurities by exchanging ions in the water, ensuring high purity levels. RO Systems employ semi-permeable membranes to filter out contaminants effectively. Distillation Systems utilize heat to vaporize water, separating it from impurities, thus providing high-purity water. Each system is essential for meeting stringent regulatory requirements and maintaining product quality in pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
