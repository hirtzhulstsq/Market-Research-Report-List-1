<p><p>The spiral wound membrane market consists of four key types: Reverse Osmosis (RO), Nanofiltration (NF), Ultrafiltration (UF), and Microfiltration (MF). RO membranes effectively remove dissolved solids and contaminants, making them ideal for desalination and water purification. NF membranes target smaller particles and specific ions, while UF membranes handle larger solutes and bacteria. MF membranes serve to separate particulate matter from liquids, providing effective clarification. Each type caters to distinct filtration needs across various industries, including water treatment and food processing.</p></p>
