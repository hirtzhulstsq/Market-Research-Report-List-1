<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aluminum Beverage Container Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DRD (Draw and Redraw) Cans</li><li>DWI (Drawn and Wall Ironed) Cans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum beverage container market features two main types: Draw and Redraw (DRD) cans and Drawn and Wall Ironed (DWI) cans. DRD cans are formed by drawing a pre-cut aluminum disc into a cup and then redrawing it to achieve the desired dimensions, making them suitable for various beverages. In contrast, DWI cans are produced through a process that not only draws but also irons the can walls, resulting in a thinner, more uniform structure, ideal for sleek designs and lightweight packaging.</p></p>
