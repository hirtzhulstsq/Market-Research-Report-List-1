<p><p>Thin-film amorphous silicon solar cells are categorized into three main market types: single-junction, dual-junction, and multi-junction. Single-junction cells consist of one amorphous silicon layer and are simpler and less expensive, but have lower efficiency. Dual-junction cells combine two layers to capture a broader spectrum of sunlight, enhancing efficiency. Multi-junction cells stack several layers to maximize light absorption and efficiency, making them suitable for specialized applications despite higher production costs. Each type serves different energy needs and market segments.</p></p>
