<p><p>The photovoltaic metallization paste market caters primarily to two types of solar cells: BSF (Back-Surface Field) solar cells and PERC (Passivated Emitter Rear Cell) solar cells. BSF solar cells enhance efficiency by reflecting unabsorbed light back into the cell, while PERC cells incorporate a passivation layer that reduces recombination losses. Both types require specialized metallization pastes for effective electrical contact and optimal performance, thereby driving innovations in formulation and application techniques within the photovoltaic industry.</p></p>
