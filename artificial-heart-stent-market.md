<p><strong>The Artificial Heart Stent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare-metal Stent (BMS)</li><li>Drug-eluting Stent (DES)</li><li>Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffold (BVS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial heart stent market comprises three primary types: Bare-metal Stents (BMS), Drug-eluting Stents (DES), and Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffolds (BVS). BMS are simple metal tubes providing structural support but without drug coating. DES are advanced stents that release medication to prevent restenosis and improve outcomes. BVS are innovative, temporary scaffolds that dissolve over time, minimizing long-term complications and promoting vascular healing. Together, these stent types cater to diverse patient needs and advancements in cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
