<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Clot Management Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neurovascular Embolectomy Devices</li><li>Embolectomy Balloon Catheters</li><li>Percutaneous Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis Devices</li><li>Inferior Vena Cava Filters (IVCF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clot Management Devices market comprises several key types aimed at addressing vascular blockages. Neurovascular embolectomy devices are utilized for removing clots from the brain's blood vessels. Embolectomy balloon catheters are designed to physically remove clots via balloon inflation. Percutaneous thrombectomy devices provide minimally invasive options for thrombus extraction. Catheter-directed thrombolysis devices deliver thrombolytic agents directly to the clot. Finally, inferior vena cava filters (IVCF) are implanted to capture clots and prevent them from traveling to the lungs.</p></p>
