<p><ul><li>Logic Output</li><li>Analog Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflective optical sensor market is categorized into two main types based on output: logic output and analog output. Logic output sensors provide a binary signal indicating the presence or absence of an object, commonly used in applications like security systems and automation. In contrast, analog output sensors deliver a continuous voltage or current signal proportional to the distance of the detected object, making them ideal for precise measurement tasks in industrial processes. Each type serves distinct operational needs across various sectors.</p></p>
