<p><ul><li>Non-Woven</li><li>Woven</li><li>Knitted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geotextiles market is categorized into three main types: non-woven, woven, and knitted. Non-woven geotextiles are made from random fibers bonded together, offering high permeability and soil separation. Woven geotextiles are constructed by interlacing fibers, providing strength and durability for applications like erosion control and reinforcement. Knitted geotextiles involve interlocking yarns, ensuring flexibility and elasticity, suitable for filtration and drainage systems. Each type serves distinct applications based on mechanical properties, permeability, and soil interaction.</p></p>
