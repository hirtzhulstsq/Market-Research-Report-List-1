<p><ul><li>Air Acetylene Welding</li><li>Oxy hydrogen Welding</li><li>Oxy Acetylene Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas welding market comprises several key types, including Air Acetylene Welding, Oxy-Hydrogen Welding, and Oxy-Acetylene Welding. Air Acetylene Welding utilizes a mixture of air and acetylene for lower-temperature applications, ideal for lighter materials. Oxy-Hydrogen Welding combines hydrogen and oxygen gases, producing a clean flame suitable for precision work. Oxy-Acetylene Welding employs a combination of oxygen and acetylene, generating high temperatures for melting and joining metals, making it the most common method in various industrial applications.</p></p>
