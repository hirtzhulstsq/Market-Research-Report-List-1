<p><p>Patient Derived Xenograft (PDX) models are innovative research tools that involve implanting human tumor tissues into immunocompromised mice. PDX models have become invaluable for evaluating drug efficacy, resistance mechanisms, and treatment outcomes, making them a cornerstone in oncological research.</p><p>The Patient Derived Xenograft Models Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period.
