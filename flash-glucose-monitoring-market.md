<p><p>Flash glucose monitoring encompasses two main market types: implantable and wearable monitoring systems. Implantable monitoring systems involve devices inserted under the skin, providing continuous glucose readings with minimal user intervention, ideal for long-term management. In contrast, wearable monitoring systems, such as patches or sensors, are externally attached to the skin, allowing users to easily access glucose data in real-time. Both types enhance diabetes management by offering convenient, accurate, and timely glucose monitoring, thereby improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
