<p><p>The hard coal market is primarily divided into Lump Anthracite and Anthracite Fines. Lump Anthracite consists of large, solid chunks that are highly valued for their high carbon content and heat output, often used in heating and industrial applications. Anthracite Fines, on the other hand, are smaller particles that result from the processing of lump coal. They are typically utilized in various industrial processes and as a fuel source, although they may command lower prices due to their size and lower heating efficiency.</p></p>
