<p><strong>The Baggage Transport and Sortation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID Baggage Transport and Sortation System</li><li>Barcode Baggage Transport and Sortation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Baggage Transport and Sortation System market is divided into two primary types: RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) and Barcode systems. RFID systems enhance efficiency and accuracy by using radio waves to automatically identify and track luggage throughout the airport, reducing handling errors and improving passenger experience. Conversely, Barcode systems rely on printed barcodes scanned at various checkpoints, which, while effective, can be slower and more prone to human error. Both systems aim to streamline baggage handling and ensure timely delivery to passengers.</p></p>
