<p><p>The Hydrogen Peroxide Disinfection System market is primarily divided into two types: Vaporized Type and Fogging Type. Vaporized Type systems utilize high-concentration hydrogen peroxide, which is vaporized to create a mist that effectively disinfects surfaces and air in enclosed spaces. Fogging Type systems generate a fine aerosol of hydrogen peroxide solution, enabling thorough penetration into all areas, including hard-to-reach spots. Both methods are effective for sterilization in various settings, including healthcare, food processing, and industrial environments.</p></p>
