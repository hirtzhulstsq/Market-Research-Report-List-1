<p><p>The earmuffs market can be divided into two main types: standard headband style earmuffs and wrap-around earmuffs. Standard headband style earmuffs typically feature a fixed headband that holds the ear cups in place, providing a secure fit for noise protection. In contrast, wrap-around earmuffs have a flexible design that conforms to the shape of the head, ensuring comfort during extended use. Both types are popular in various settings, including industrial workplaces, shooting ranges, and recreational activities, prioritizing noise reduction and user comfort.</p></p>
