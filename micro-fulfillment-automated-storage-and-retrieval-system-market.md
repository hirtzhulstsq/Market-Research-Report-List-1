<p><p>Micro-Fulfillment Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) are categorized into two main types: single-mast and multi-mast systems. Single-mast systems utilize a single vertical column for storage and retrieval, maximizing space efficiency in compact environments, making them suitable for smaller operations. In contrast, multi-mast systems feature multiple vertical columns, allowing for higher storage capacity and faster retrieval rates, ideal for larger facilities. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve order fulfillment speed in e-commerce and retail settings.</p></p>
